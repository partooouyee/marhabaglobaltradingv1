COMPANY OVERVIEW

Trusted Global Trading Partner

PT Marhaba Global Trading is a leading export-import company established in August 2025, operating from Jakarta, Indonesia. We are part of a larger network spanning Afghanistan, China, Nepal, India, and Pakistan.

Our mission is to bridge international commerce through professional service, competitive pricing, and secure transactions. We prioritize transparency, integrity, and excellence in every business relationship.

Diverse Industries, Unified Excellence

We specialize in every key sectors, each backed by years of industry expertise and a commitment to quality. We focus on four key sectors where Indonesian resources meet high global demand, ensuring quality at every transaction.

From industrial equipment and textile products to hospitality services and agricultural commodities, we deliver reliable trading solutions tailored to global market needs. Our network of trusted partners and suppliers enables us to maintain consistent quality, competitive pricing, and efficient cross-border operations across every sector we serve.
